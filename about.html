About Us

We're transforming inland shipping in Europe, making it more accessible for everyone involved in the industry.

Our Mission

Strengthen inland shipping in Europe. We aim to create a more efficient and cost-effective transport network. Whether you're a company looking to send goods faster and cheaper, or a barge owner aiming to transport more cargo, we're here to support you.

With our groundbreaking platform, we facilitate a seamless exchange of freight and services, transforming the inland barging sector. At the heart of our mission are three core values: sustainability, competitiveness, and customer-focused service.
